Drop your images here 🌸
========================

Put any images you'd like on the site into THIS folder. For example:
  • the photo that shows on the camera "screen"
  • your polaroid photos
  • the album cover for the iPod
  • photos for the "photos!" folder
  • screenshots / thumbnails for your projects

Any filenames are fine (beach.jpg, poppy-field.png, reel-cover.jpg, etc.).

When you're done, just tell Claude "I added the images" — Claude will
see what's here and wire each one into the right spot on the desktop.

Tip: PNG files with a transparent background look best for the camera,
iPod, and polaroid frames. Regular photos (JPG/PNG) are perfect for the
folders and polaroids.
